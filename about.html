Who we are

Investing built around your goals

SuperMatrix is a wealth management platform of Sarthi Financial Services Private Limited (CIN: U67190DL2009PTC187401), an AMFI-Registered Mutual Fund Distributor. Combining technology with professional service, SuperMatrix simplifies investing by offering a seamless, transparent, and goal-based investment experience with access to a diverse range of financial solutions.

At SuperMatrix, we believe that every investment journey should begin with a clear financial goal. Our platform is built around a goal-based investing approach, enabling investors to plan for milestones such as buying a home, funding education, planning retirement, creating wealth, or building a financial legacy.

We leverage technology to simplify the entire investment process—from onboarding and paperless transactions to portfolio tracking and investment monitoring—making investing convenient and accessible. At the same time, our experienced professionals assist investors throughout their investment journey by helping them understand investment options and complete their transactions smoothly.

SuperMatrix offers access to a wide range of mutual fund schemes from leading Asset Management Companies (AMCs), allowing investors to choose solutions that align with their financial goals, investment horizon, and risk appetite.

Backed by the values of trust, transparency, professionalism, and innovation, SuperMatrix is committed to making investing easier, more organised, and more accessible while helping investors stay focused on their long-term financial aspirations.
