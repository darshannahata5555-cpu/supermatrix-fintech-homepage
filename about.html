Who we are

Investing built around your goals

SuperMatrix is a wealth management initiative of Sarthi Financial Services Private Limited (CIN: U67190DL2009PTC187401), presented as an AMFI-Registered Mutual Fund Distributor. Combining digital tools with professional service, SuperMatrix aims to make goal-based investing more organised and understandable.

We believe every investment journey should begin with a clear financial goal. Our goal-based approach helps investors plan for milestones such as buying a home, funding education, retirement, wealth creation, or building a financial legacy.

The current website provides educational product information, calculators, FAQs, and access to human support. Confirmed onboarding, transaction, and portfolio-access routes will be linked once production integrations are available.

Access with purpose

SuperMatrix helps investors understand mutual fund categories and financial solutions in the context of their goals, investment horizon, and risk appetite.

Our aim is to make investing easier to understand while clearly presenting risks, costs, and important disclosures.
