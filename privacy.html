Privacy

Website privacy notice

This notice explains the limited information handled through the current SuperMatrix website.

Information you choose to provide

If you contact SuperMatrix, you may provide your name, email address, phone number, and query. The website enquiry tool prepares an email in your email application; you decide whether to send it.

Local website data

The fixed FAQ chatbot does not transmit or store chat messages. Calculator inputs are processed in your browser. Administrative preview settings, when used, are stored in that browser’s local storage.

How information may be used

Information you send may be used to respond to your request, provide service assistance, maintain necessary records, and meet applicable legal or regulatory obligations.

Sharing and security

Information should only be shared where required to provide the requested service, comply with law, or work with authorised service providers under appropriate safeguards. Do not submit passwords, OTPs, card details, or other highly sensitive credentials through the website.

Your choices

You may request correction or deletion of information where applicable by emailing support@supermatrix.in.

Review status: This operational website notice should be reviewed against the organisation’s final legal, retention, vendor, and cookie practices before production use.
