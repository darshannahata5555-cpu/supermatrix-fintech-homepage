Grievance redressal

Use the following route for service complaints or unresolved concerns.

Step 1 — Contact SuperMatrix

Email support@supermatrix.in or call 022-26528671 / 72. Include your name, registered contact details, folio or reference number where relevant, a clear description, and supporting documents. Never send passwords or OTPs.

Step 2 — Formal escalation

If the issue is unresolved, write to the designated grievance officer. The officer’s confirmed name, email, telephone number, and response timeline must be inserted after business confirmation.

Step 3 — External escalation

Depending on the issue, investors may contact the concerned AMC/RTA and use applicable regulatory grievance mechanisms. Visit SEBI SCORES and the SEBI Investor website for current guidance.

Complaint tracking

A secure complaint reference and tracking system should be connected when the production backend is available. The current static website does not store complaints.

Please retain copies of correspondence and supporting documents relating to your complaint.
