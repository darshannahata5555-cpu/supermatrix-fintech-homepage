Important information

Disclosures and risk information

Understand the capacity in which SuperMatrix operates and the limitations of website information.

Mutual fund distribution

SuperMatrix presents itself as an AMFI-registered Mutual Fund Distributor. The confirmed legal entity name, ARN, validity, and applicable EUIN information must be inserted after documentary verification.

Regular and Direct Plans

Mutual Fund Distributors facilitate Regular Plans and may receive trail commission from AMCs. Direct Plans are available directly through AMCs and generally have a lower expense ratio because distributor commission is not included.

No assured returns

Mutual funds are market-linked. Values can rise or fall and past performance does not guarantee future performance. No calculator output or website illustration is an assurance.

Suitability and scheme documents

Before investing, consider goals, horizon, risk tolerance, liquidity, taxation, expense ratio, exit load, Riskometer, SID, KIM, and other applicable documents.

Calculator limitations

Results use simplified mathematical assumptions and may exclude tax, loads, expense ratios, cash-flow timing differences, inflation changes, and market volatility.

PMS information

PMS content is general information only. Any actual offering must identify the registered portfolio manager, strategy documents, fees, risks, eligibility, and regulatory details before solicitation.

Mutual Fund investments are subject to market risks. Please read all scheme-related documents carefully before investing.
