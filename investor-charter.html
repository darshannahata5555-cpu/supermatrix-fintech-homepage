Investor Charter and good practices

A practical summary with links to authoritative investor resources.

Investor rights

Receive material product information, understand risks and costs, obtain applicable transaction records, have personal information handled appropriately, and access grievance mechanisms.

Investor responsibilities

Provide accurate KYC and account information, read scheme documents, review statements, keep contact and nomination details current, and promptly report discrepancies.

Protect yourself

Never share passwords, PINs, OTPs, or remote-device access. Verify payment instructions and do not rely on promises of guaranteed market-linked returns.

Authoritative resources

Read the current SEBI Investor Charter, visit AMFI, and use SCORES where applicable.
